About

Combined with my technical and financial background, this will allow me to approach asset management with a well-rounded perspective. Tech-wise, I'm skilled with industry mainstays like AutoCAD and Revit. I also have a keen interest in architectural visualization, using cutting-edge tools like Blender and UnrealEngine. Driven by a strong passion for learning and innovation, I approach every project with a commitment to excellence and making a real difference.
